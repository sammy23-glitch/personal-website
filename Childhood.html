My Childhood Journey

Early Life & Curiosity

From a young age, I was always curious about how things work, especially electronic devices and computers. I enjoyed exploring technology and learning new things, which later inspired my interest in software engineering.

Love for Sports

Growing up, I actively participated in sports such as bicycle riding and racing bikes. These activities helped me develop discipline, focus, endurance, and teamwork. Sports taught me the importance of consistency and determination.

Leadership Experience

During my childhood and school years, I took part in leadership roles where I learned responsibility, communication, and decision-making. These experiences helped shape my confidence and ability to work with others.
